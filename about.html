关于铭纬

最世界最大的LED灯饰制造中心之一

关于铭纬发展历程 资质证书 车间概况 首页 > 公司简介 > 关于铭纬

最世界最大的LED灯饰制造中心之一

One of the most world's largest LED lighting manufacturing center

中山市古镇铭纬显示屏厂是一家专业从事LED电子显示屏研制、生产、销售和服务的高科技企业，为国内 外客户提供户内、户外全彩系列显示屏的OEM、ODM服务。产品包括LED室内/户外全彩屏、单/双色屏、条形信息屏、异形屏等系列产品。

作为最大的LED灯饰制造中心之一，我们拥有现代化的生产线和世界一流的制造设备，及相应的检测设备，真正实现了产业化经营，并形成了完整的产品系列。

在IS09001：2000的质量管理体系下，使公司所有产品品质在每一道工序中均得到有效控制。我们拥有一支高素质的技术研发、安装队伍和一批在LED显示屏行业中富有经验的管理人员。通过长期的积累已形成了一整套完整的产品质量保证体系和安装售后服务保障体系，确保客户得到高可靠性的最新产品，享受到最优质的安装售后服务。

Zhongshan Ming-wai display Factory is a professional LED electronic display research and high-tech production, sales and service for domestic and foreign customers with indoor, OEM outdoor full-color display, ODM service. Products include LED indoor / outdoor full color, single / two-color screen, strip information screens, special screens and other products.

As one of the largest LED lighting manufacturing center, we have modern production lines and world class manufacturing facilities, and the corresponding testing equipment, truly industrial management, and formed a complete product line.

In the IS09001: 2000 quality management system under the company all product quality at every step, have been effectively controlled. We have a highly qualified technical research and development, installation team and a group of rich experience in the LED display industry managers. Through long-term accumulation has formed a complete set of product quality assurance system and installation service assurance system to ensure that customers receive the latest high-reliability products, enjoy the best quality installation service.
